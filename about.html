Jigsaw

Recreation of 5 minigames from the longest running gameshow in Serbia called 'Slagalica'. Minigames original names are (in the same order as in my recreated version): 'Moj broj', 'Skocko', 'Spojnice', 'Ko zna zna', 'Asocijacije'.

Tic-Tac-Toe

A classic paper and pencil game whose early variations can be traced back to Roman Empire.
I made a small algorithm that let's a player play against CPU. The algorithm is written in such a way that CPU first checks if a center field is taken since taking that field enhances the chance of winning. If not, CPU will pick another position. After that CPU's will search if there is a field which, if he chose, would bring him victory. If such field does not exist, he places 'O' in a field within a direction where player did not interfere yet. If a player is close to winning CPU will try to stop him by placing 'O' in a player's potential third field.
